Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kinder (3-6 years) is a critical stage where children explore, learn foundational skills, develop social-emotional abilities, and build curiosity, creativity, and confidence for lifelong growth.

Elementary School

Elementary school lays the foundation for learning, focusing on core subjects, critical thinking, social skills, and character development, fostering curiosity and preparing students for lifelong success.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school (10-16 years) bridges childhood and adolescence, fostering critical thinking, independence, social development, and academic growth, preparing students for higher education and future challenges.
